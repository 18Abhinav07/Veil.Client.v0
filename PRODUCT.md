# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Professional Web3 users seeking a secure, private payment wallet. They want to initiate private or public payments quickly and cleanly, under varied ambient lighting (on the go or in office settings), demanding absolute clarity on local cryptographic key vault creation and password security.

## Product Purpose
Veil is a secure, client-side encrypted wallet app allowing users to make private payments (and public payments) without exposing cleartext wallet secrets. Success is defined by an intuitive local vault setup, quick secure unlocking, and a clean, high-performance payment experience.

## Brand Personality
- Cinematic tech-minimalism
- Professional clarity
- Flowing precision (professional motion and animation)

## Anti-references
- Neon-on-black generic crypto dashboards
- Cluttered, information-dense tables and cards-in-cards UI
- Fragile layouts or slow/abrupt transitions

## Design Principles
1. **Cryptographic visual intuition**: Keep client-side vault processes transparently explained and beautifully simple, shielding the user from technical noise.
2. **Minimalist pacing & flow**: Dedicate screen real estate to the primary action; utilize clean progressive disclosure (tabs, skeletons) to prevent visual cognitive overload.
3. **Professional motion signature**: Emphasize fluid, high-performance animations and transitions to feel highly responsive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA contrast compliance for light theme with dark accents
- Graceful animation scaling for users preferring reduced motion
- Distinct focus outlines and keyboard-navigable forms
